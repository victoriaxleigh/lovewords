LoveWords Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

LoveWords ("we", "the app") is a word game you play with a partner or friend. We keep data collection to the minimum needed to run the game. This policy explains what we collect, why, and your choices.

Information we collect

  • Account information — your email address and the display name you choose when you sign up. Your email is used to sign you in and to let people you know invite you to a game.
  • Game data — the games you play, moves, scores, and any in-game notes or messages you send to the other player.
  • Notification token — if you allow notifications, a push token so we can tell you when it's your turn or a message arrives. You can turn notifications off at any time in your device settings.

We do not collect your contacts, location, advertising identifiers, or browsing activity, and we do not use third-party analytics or advertising trackers.

How we use your information

  • To create and secure your account and sign you in.
  • To run your games and deliver notes/messages to the other player.
  • To send the push notifications you opt into (turn alerts, messages, nudges).

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it for advertising.

Service providers

We use trusted providers only to operate the app:

  • Supabase — stores your account and game data.
  • Netlify — hosts the app and its notification service.
  • Apple / Google / Expo push services — deliver notifications to your device.

These providers process data on our behalf and only as needed to provide their service.

Data retention and deletion

We keep your data while your account is active. You can permanently delete your account and its game data at any time from Settings → Delete account in the app. When you delete your account, your profile and the games tied to it are removed.

Children

LoveWords is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
